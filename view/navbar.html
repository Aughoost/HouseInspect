About Inspect A Home

Qualified & Experienced Inspectors

Our inspectors are licensed building practitioners and experienced builders, trained to national standards. We provide detailed, clear, and reliable reports so you understand exactly what’s going on with your property.

Efficient, Clear & Customer-Focused

We aim to book inspections within 48 hours and deliver written reports the same day or the next. Our process is streamlined: we liaise with agents or vendors on your behalf, and present findings in plain language—no jargon, just clarity.

Why Choose Us?

  • Comprehensive visual inspections of all key areas of the home
  • Written reports with photographs, commentary & recommendations
  • Friendly, responsive support before & after the inspection
  • Commitment to honesty, integrity & your best outcome
